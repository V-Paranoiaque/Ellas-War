The attacks
Classic attacks
  • You need units in your attack wave to start attack
  • You can't attack a player who is in the same alliance than you
  • You can't attack a player that your alliance has a pact with
  • You can't attack the same player more than two days in a row (anti-bashing)
  • You can attack cities that have between 50% and 200% of your field
  • You can attack cities that have a maximum 4 levels of difference with you, except if both players have a field larger or equal to 25000
  • The field rule doesn't apply if the targeted player has the diamond
  • The difference between offensive and defensive strength is used to calculate building destruction and resource plunder.
War attacks
  • The previous rule about antibashing doesn't apply anymore
  • If you have under 8.000 of field, you can attack all the players that are under 8.000 too. The level rule still applies.
  • Attacking a player via the diamond needs to repect the field rule to count as a war attack
