Strength of sanctuaries
Each day the sanctuaries lose 5 points of energy. When it's energy is going under 100, the defense of the unities keeping it will decrease.
To recharge them, you need to pay a fee based on the units defending it.

100 points and more:  100% of the defense
Under 100 points:  % according to the points
If your account becomes inactive for more than 30 days, the monster will rebel and regain its freedom.
